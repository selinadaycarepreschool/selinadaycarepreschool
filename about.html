About Us

Selina

My name is Selina Chen and I have over 15 years of experience as a childcare professional. I have been a San Francisco native for over twenty years and am proud to have been able to serve my community by educating children to become curious thinkers and upstanding citizens. My goal is to encourage and guide children to learn how to interact successfully with other children and adults, and to live together in a cooperative environment which promotes decision-making, peaceful resolution of conflicts, and respect and care for others. In doing so, I also hope to form a cooperative partnership with the parents so that we can work together to meet the needs of their children.
